ABOUT ME

I am a fully qualified and registered child and adolescent psychotherapist with a Masters degree in Psychodynamic Counselling and Psychotherapy with Children and Adolescents from Birkbeck College, University of London.

My clinical experience includes working in Primary Care clinics and charity private and school settings with children and young people aged from 4-24 years. I have worked extensively with bereavement/loss and with young people experiencing low mood and who may be at risk.

My CPD (continued professional development) training includes Working with Trauma (The Tavistock and Portman), Working with Eating Disorders and Body Dysmorphia (The Tavistock and Portman) and the year Foundation Course at The Institute of Psychoanalysis, London.

I am a registered member of the British Asssociation of Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP) and the BPC (British Psychoanalytic Council) and abide by their codes of ethics. I have full professional liability insurance cover and up to date DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) Certificate.
